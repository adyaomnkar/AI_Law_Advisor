Section 1
BNS - Short Title and Commencement
The Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023 (BNS) replaces the Indian Penal Code, 1860. It came into force on 1st July 2024. It codifies general criminal law applicable across India for offences against persons, property, the state, and public order.

Section 4
BNS - Punishment for Offences Committed Beyond India
Every person shall be liable to punishment under this Sanhita for every act or omission contrary to its provisions, of which he shall be guilty within India.

Section 63
BNS - Rape
Whoever, except in the cases provided for in subsection (2), commits rape shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment of not less than ten years, but which may extend to imprisonment for life. Rape means sexual intercourse with a woman without her consent, by force, fraud, or coercion.

Section 64
BNS - Punishment for Rape
Punishment for rape: rigorous imprisonment of not less than 10 years, may extend to life, and shall pay compensation to victim. Aggravated rape (victim below 18 years): not less than 20 years, may extend to life imprisonment or death.

Section 65
BNS - Punishment for Rape in Certain Cases
Whoever commits rape on a woman under 16 years of age shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment for not less than twenty years, but which may extend to imprisonment for life meaning imprisonment for the remainder of that person's natural life, and shall also be liable to fine.

Section 66
BNS - Sexual Intercourse by Husband upon His Wife during Separation
Sexual intercourse by a man with his own wife, the wife being separated from him under a decree of separation or under any custom or usage is punishable with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to two years.

Section 67
BNS - Sexual Intercourse by Person in Authority
Whoever, being in a position of authority or in a fiduciary relationship with the victim, commits or abets sexual intercourse, shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment of not less than five years.

Section 69
BNS - Sexual Intercourse by Deceitful Means
Whoever, by deceitful means, including false promise of marriage, employment, or promotion, induces a woman to consent to sexual intercourse shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to ten years.

Section 70
BNS - Gang Rape
When a woman is raped by one or more persons constituting a group or acting in furtherance of a common intention, each of those persons shall be deemed to have committed the offence of rape and shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than twenty years, but which may extend to imprisonment for life.

Section 74
BNS - Assault or Criminal Force to Woman with Intent to Outrage Her Modesty
Whoever assaults or uses criminal force to any woman, intending to outrage or knowing it to be likely that he will thereby outrage her modesty, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to five years.

Section 75
BNS - Sexual Harassment
Sexual harassment of the nature of unwelcome physical, verbal or non-verbal conduct of sexual nature is an offence. Punishment: Imprisonment up to 3 years, or fine, or both. Includes making sexually coloured remarks, showing pornography, making unwelcome demands for sexual favours.

Section 76
BNS - Voyeurism
Whoever watches, or captures the image of, a woman engaging in a private act in circumstances where she would usually have the expectation of not being observed, shall be punished on first conviction with imprisonment of either description for a term which shall not be less than one year but which may extend to three years.

Section 77
BNS - Stalking
Whoever follows a woman and contacts, or attempts to contact such woman to foster personal interaction repeatedly despite a clear indication of disinterest by such woman, shall be punished on first conviction with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three years.

Section 78
BNS - Word, Gesture or Act Intended to Insult the Modesty of a Woman
Whoever, intending to insult the modesty of any woman, utters any word, makes any sound or gesture, or exhibits any object, intending that such word or sound shall be heard, or that such gesture or object shall be seen by such woman, shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years.

Section 79
BNS - Kidnapping
Whoever kidnaps any person from India or from lawful guardianship shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.

Section 84
BNS - Trafficking of Persons
Trafficking of a person means the recruitment, transportation, transfer, harbouring or receipt of persons, by means of threat or use of force, for exploitation. Punishment: rigorous imprisonment not less than 7 years, may extend to 10 years. If victim is child: not less than 10 years, may extend to life imprisonment.

Section 85
BNS - Husband or Relative of Husband of a Woman Subjecting Her to Cruelty
Whoever, being the husband or the relative of the husband of a woman, subjects such woman to cruelty shall be punished with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years and shall also be liable to fine. Cruelty includes wilful conduct likely to drive the woman to suicide or cause grave physical or mental injury.

Section 86
BNS - Dowry Death
Where the death of a woman is caused by burns or bodily injury or occurs under suspicious circumstances within seven years of marriage, and it is shown that she was subjected to cruelty or harassment in connection with demand for dowry, such death shall be called dowry death, punishable with imprisonment for not less than seven years.

Section 103
BNS - Murder
Whoever causes death with intention or knowledge that the act is likely to cause death, commits murder. Punishment: Death penalty or imprisonment for life, and fine. Exceptions: private defence, sudden provocation.

Section 109
BNS - Abetment of Suicide
If any person commits suicide, whoever abets the commission of such suicide, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to ten years. If victim is minor, insane, or under influence: imprisonment not less than 5 years.

Section 111
BNS - Organized Crime
Any continuing unlawful activity including extortion, land grabbing, contract killing, kidnapping, robbery, drug trafficking, human trafficking, cyber crime, carried on by any person or group by use of violence or threat of violence constitutes organized crime. Punishment: If death of victim: death or life imprisonment. Otherwise: not less than 5 years.

Section 113
BNS - Terrorist Act
Whoever commits a terrorist act shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than five years but which may extend to imprisonment for life. If act causes death of any person: death penalty or imprisonment for life. Terrorist act means any act done with intent to threaten the unity, integrity, sovereignty, security of India.

Section 115
BNS - Voluntarily Causing Hurt
Whoever voluntarily causes hurt shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to one year, or with fine which may extend to ten thousand rupees, or with both.

Section 117
BNS - Grievous Hurt
Whoever voluntarily causes grievous hurt shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine. Includes permanent disfiguration, loss of limb or organ.

Section 124
BNS - Acid Attack
Whoever causes permanent or partial damage or deformity to, or burns or maims or disfigures or disables, any part or parts of the body of a person by throwing acid on or administering acid to that person shall be punished with rigorous imprisonment for a term which shall not be less than ten years but which may extend to imprisonment for life.

Section 130
BNS - Culpable Homicide Not Amounting to Murder
Punishment: Imprisonment for life or imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to ten years, and fine.

Section 140
BNS - Wrongful Confinement
Whoever wrongfully confines any person shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a term which may extend to one year, or with fine which may extend to five thousand rupees, or with both.

Section 189
BNS - Sedition and Endangering Sovereignty
Acts endangering sovereignty, unity and integrity of India: whoever commits, excites, or attempts to excite secession, armed rebellion, subversive activities, feelings of separatist activities shall be punished with imprisonment for life or imprisonment which may extend to seven years.

Section 196
BNS - Promoting Enmity Between Groups
Whoever commits any act which is prejudicial to the maintenance of harmony between different religious, racial, language or regional groups and which disturbs or is likely to disturb the public tranquillity, shall be punished with imprisonment which may extend to three years, or fine, or both.

Section 275
BNS - Theft
Whoever intending to take dishonestly any moveable property out of the possession of any person without that person's consent, moves that property in order to such taking, is said to commit theft. Punishment: imprisonment which may extend to three years, or fine, or both.

Section 304
BNS - Cheating
Whoever cheats shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three years, or with fine, or with both. Cheating by impersonation: imprisonment which may extend to five years.

Section 306
BNS - Cheating with Knowledge that Wrongful Loss May Ensue
Whoever cheats with the knowledge that he is likely thereby to cause wrongful loss to a person whose interest in the transaction to which the cheating relates, he was bound either by law, or by a legal contract, to protect, shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to three years.

Section 308
BNS - Cheating and Dishonestly Inducing Delivery of Property
Whoever cheats and thereby dishonestly induces the person deceived to deliver any property to any person shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years, or with fine, or with both.

Section 316
BNS - Criminal Breach of Trust
Whoever, being in any manner entrusted with property, or with dominion over property, dishonestly misappropriates or converts to his own use that property shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to seven years, and shall also be liable to fine.

Section 318
BNS - Forgery
Whoever commits forgery shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both. Forgery for purpose of cheating: imprisonment which may extend to seven years.

Section 319
BNS - Using a Forged Document
Whoever fraudulently or dishonestly uses as genuine any document which he knows or has reason to believe to be a forged document, shall be punished in the same manner as if he had forged such document.

Section 336
BNS - Mischief
Whoever with intent to cause, or knowing that he is likely to cause, wrongful loss or damage to the public or to any person, causes the destruction of any property, or any such change in any property as destroys or diminishes its value or utility, commits mischief. Punishment: imprisonment which may extend to six months.

Section 351
BNS - Criminal Intimidation
Whoever threatens another with any injury to his person, reputation or property, or to the person or reputation of any one in whom that person is interested, with intent to cause alarm to that person shall be punished with imprisonment of either description for a term which may extend to two years, or with fine, or with both.
